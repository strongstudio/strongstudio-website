Bio: 

Matthew founded Strong Studio in 2006. Prior to establishing the company, he served as the Senior Creative Director for Madison Square Garden, overseeing and creating season identities for the New York Knicks and New York Rangers as well as designs for Radio City Music Hall. He began his professional career at SME Branding where he helped develop and extend brands for the NFL, NBA and MLB.

In 1999, Matthew graduated from Pratt Institute with highest honors from the Communications Design department where he has been teaching Package Design and Graphic Design since 2004. Matthew is also a member of the American Institute of Graphic Arts (AIGA).
